About This Page

Due to the sheer size of the dataset, I have not broken down my analysis by year. If you are interested in seeing how the two themes I highlighted - gender and nationality - changed over space and time, I have included story maps on this page. There is also a main map that allows you to search by Composer, Gender, Nationality, or Work, so you can explore the data on your own. You'll notice that much of the data is clustered around Europe and is overwhelmingly male, both of which track with the findings I present throughout this site.

Gender by Season

Click the arrows to move through each season. Click the colors on the legend to highlight each gender on the map.

Nationality by Season

These maps are filtered to show just the top 10 nationalities of composers for each season. I did this for readability - the top nationalities were the same even up to the top 20, and having only 10 colors makes it easier to see patterns than 20 colors. As in the maps above, you can click on each nationality to see it highlighted on the map.
